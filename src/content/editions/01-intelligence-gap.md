Pakistan does not lack economic news. It lacks economic *understanding* delivered on time, with a view, to the people who have to act on it.

Every week, the numbers move — the rupee, oil benchmarks, fuel prices, the policy rate, commodity cycles. The daily press reports them. Op-eds interpret them through the same well-worn frames. Almost nobody connects the global mechanics to the concrete, local consequence: what this means for your costs, your margins, your decisions this week.

## What this is

The Pulse Paper closes that gap. Each weekly edition is one focused brief — what moved, what's developing, and what to do about it. Not "monitor the situation." Real decisions, with thresholds and dates.

## Who it's for

The people who make decisions with money: CFOs, procurement and supply-chain heads, investors, business owners, and analysts who need the signal without the noise.
